About Us

We are Tejas, Aryan, and Aditya — first-year Computer Science students at MUJ. As freshers, we quickly realized how unorganized and outdated the mess system felt when it came to accessing menus, sharing feedback, or making collective decisions. So, we built this platform to fix that. Our website gives students a central hub for everything mess-related — from viewing daily and weekly menus to participating in mess food voting, giving feedback, and staying updated with any important announcements or changes. This project is more than just a website. It’s our way of making student life smoother and giving everyone a voice in what they eat. Made by students, for students — and we’re just getting started.
