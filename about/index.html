About Us

We are Tejas, Aryan, and Aditya — first-year Computer Science students at MUJ. As freshers, we quickly realized how unorganized and outdated the mess system felt.

So, we built THIS PLATFORM to fix that.

Our website gives students a central hub for everything mess-related — from viewing menus to participating in mess food voting, giving feedback, and staying updated with any important announcements or changes.
Made by students, for students — and we’re just getting started.
